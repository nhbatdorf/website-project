Pasta

Ingredients:

  • Spagetti
  • Ground Beef
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Oregano

Directions:

  • Brown Beef
  • Drain Fat
  • Heat Tomatoes on Low
  • Stir in Beef and Oregano
  • Boil Water
  • Cook Spagetti for 5 Minutes
  • Drain and Top with Sauce and Cheese
